A Gift, A Dream, A Kitchen

Some dreams take
a lifetime to come true.

For nearly 38 years, Appa has given his life to the art of South Indian cooking — starting out at the very first Sagar Ratna, then spending over three decades at the iconic Kutty's on Jantar Mantar Road, Connaught Place, feeding countless food lovers along the way.

When his son and daughter-in-law asked if he was finally ready to slow down, his answer was simple.

"My relaxation is being in the kitchen."

So instead of asking him to rest, they gave him a kitchen of his own — a place to cook at his own pace, serve from his heart, and keep doing what he has always loved most. That is how Appa's Kitchen was born, right here in Raj Nagar.
